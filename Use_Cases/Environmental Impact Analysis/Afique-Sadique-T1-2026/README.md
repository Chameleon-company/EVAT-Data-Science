# Electric Vehicles for Sustainability 
The core objective of this study is to estimate the total amount of emissions produced by small vehicles in Australia. While the number of small vehicles is known, the corresponding emission levels are not directly available. To address this gap, prior knowledge derived from an emission dataset is incorporated into the analysis. The mean and standard deviation of this prior distribution are used to model and predict the total emissions generated by Australia’s small vehicle fleet. By examining historical emission patterns, the study also visualises long term trends and highlights the potential risks associated with increasing emissions if sustainability measures are not prioritised. Additionally, the analysis considers the positive impact of electric vehicle adoption in reducing overall emissions and supporting environmentally responsible transport planning.

## Dataset
- Analyse small vehicles [Real World Comparison among ICE and EV in Australia] - AAA index
- Greenhouse emissions [Emission Level] - Kaggle
- Footprint Measurements [CO2 footprint]- Environmental Protection Agency, USA
